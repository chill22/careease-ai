Good news: **You don’t have to be poor to get free help in 2025.**

Here are the **7 real programs** that gave caregivers an average of 42 days off last year:

### 1. ARCH National Respite Network (up to 14 days FREE)
   Even if your income is $150k+. Call 919-490-5577 → they find funding in your state.

### 2. Area Agency on Aging “Emergency Respite”
   Many states offer 72-hour emergency breaks. Google: “[your county] Area Agency on Aging respite”

### 3. Alzheimer’s Association Comfort Care (dementia-specific)
   Up to 30 hours/month FREE if your loved one has a dementia diagnosis. Call 1-800-272-3900

### 4. Veteran Family Caregiver Program (VA)
   If either of you ever served → up to 30 days/year of in-home help. Call 1-855-260-3274

### 5. Local churches & synagogues
   Many have “Stephen Ministry” or volunteer companion programs — 4-hour breaks every week.

### 6. State Lifespan Respite Grants
   38 states have them. Google: “[your state] Lifespan Respite Voucher”

### 7. Your local senior center
   Many offer 3–6 hour adult day programs for $0–$25/day (sliding scale).
